About

My name is Remonda, and I am a nature enthusiast. I created Figeographie to showcase the extraordinary world that we live in.

The name “Figeographie” was inspired by the first deliberate photo I ever took — a fig on the ground during a walk to school with my sister. Each day, we would pass a beautiful fig tree bursting with ripe fruit. Many would fall, and we’d look for the uncracked ones to snack on as we walked.

That fig, and that moment, captured the joy and sweetness I find in nature. It became the symbol of my love for photography — especially of plants.

I hope this site helps you discover a little sweetness in the world around you, too.

“Look deep into nature, and then you will understand everything better.”
