How We Originated

In June 2025, Remonda Hassan created Figeographie. It was originally meant to showcase her nature photography. The name “Figeographie” was inspired by the first deliberate photo she took — a fig on the ground while walking to school with her sister.

Why We Proceeded

While coding the site, she remembered her article on the fashion industry’s impact on global clean water, and decided to link an articles page. She couldn't just let go of an opportunity to help the most vulnerable in the world.

Where We Are Now

Today, Figeographie is run by volunteers who combine nature photography, environmental articles, and a charity collaboration with Embrace Relief for water wells in Sub-Saharan Africa to help quench the thirst of billions deprived of this basic need.
