About Figeographie

In June 2025, Remonda Hassan created Figeographie. It was originally meant to showcase her nature photography.

1/4

The name “Figeographie” was inspired by the first deliberate photo she took — a fig on the ground while walking to school with her sister.

2/4

While coding the site, she remembered her article on the fashion industry’s impact on global clean water, and decided to link an articles page.

3/4

Today, Figeographie combines nature photography, environmental articles, and charity collaboration with Embrace Relief for water wells in Sub-Saharan Africa.

4/4
